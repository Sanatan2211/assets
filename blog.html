What is a Marketing Funnel: Full Guide!

  • San
  • Dec 22, 2022

A marketing funnel is a visual representation of how a consumer moves through various stages of the buying cycle. In other words, it shows how someone becomes aware of your brand and then converts into a customer. The four basic steps in any marketing funnel are: awareness, consideration, action and loyalty.
